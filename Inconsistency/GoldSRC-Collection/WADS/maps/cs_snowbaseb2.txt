Snowbase - Hostage Rescue
by Veldrin (veldrin@nihilistic.com)

Background: Terrorists have infiltrated 
a remote nuclear missile silo using a
highjacked APC.  Their intent is to launch
the missiles.

Counter-Terrorists: Enter the base and
rescue the hostages at any cost.  They
hold the missile launch codes.

Terrorists: Prevent Counter-Terrorist
force from rescuing the hostages.

Other Notes: There are 4 hostages in 
the mission.
